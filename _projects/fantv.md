This was my first software engineering job straight out of college and here is where I grew a lot. I learned about programming patters such as Observser/Subscriber, MVC, coding to interfaces rather than to implementations, and much more. FanTV was a startup located in San Matero with an awesome culture where my co-workers and I would go out to eat together every lunch and go out to happy. Many of us are still in contact today.

### Android Development

At FanTV I worked on a custom hardware device that was running on Android OS with a custom OpenGL UI framework layer. I worked on the main application as well as other system services.

### Watch History

I implemented watch history which is the feature that allows users to resume a program from where they left off. It was a great project for me to learn about video players, REST APIs, and other design patterns. 

### Analytics

I wrote code that captured the analytics necessary to fullfill our contract with our partners . I was a key contact person for handling the details of the analytics being reported by the application.
